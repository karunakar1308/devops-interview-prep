## ðŸ’¼ During Interview

### First Impressions (First 2 Minutes)
- [ ] Smile and make eye contact when you join
- [ ] Greet interviewers warmly: "Hi! Thanks so much for having me."
- [ ] If they ask "How are you?": "I'm doing great, thank you! Excited to be here."
- [ ] Sit up straight, look engaged

### Active Interview Mode
- [ ] Use STAR format for every behavioral question
- [ ] Pause 2-3 seconds before answering (it's OK to think!)
- [ ] Use "I" not "we" to show your contributions
- [ ] Mention specific tools from job description
- [ ] Ask clarifying questions if needed
- [ ] Take brief notes when they speak
- [ ] Show enthusiasm about the role

### Technical Depth Questions
- [ ] If you know it: explain clearly with examples
- [ ] If unsure: "Let me think... I believe [approach]... but I'd want to verify..."
- [ ] If you don't know: "I haven't worked with that specifically, but here's how I'd approach it based on my experience with [similar thing]..."
- [ ] Draw parallels to your experience

### Body Language
- [ ] Maintain good posture
- [ ] Smile naturally
- [ ] Nod when listening
- [ ] Use hand gestures when explaining technical concepts
- [ ] Vary your tone (don't be monotone)

## ðŸ§˜ Stress Management Techniques

### If You Feel Nervous
**Box Breathing (repeat 3-4 times):**
1. Breathe in for 4 counts
2. Hold for 4 counts
3. Breathe out for 4 counts
4. Hold for 4 counts

**Power Pose** (2 minutes before interview):
- Stand up straight
- Hands on hips or arms raised in victory
- This actually changes body chemistry and reduces stress

**Positive Self-Talk:**
- "I am prepared."
- "I have valuable experience."
- "They called ME for an interview - they already think I'm qualified."
- "I belong in this conversation."

## ðŸš¨ Emergency Scenarios

### Technical Failures

**Camera Stops Working:**
- "I apologize - I'm having camera issues. Give me one moment..."
- Switch to backup device or phone
- Continue with audio only if needed

**Internet Drops:**
- Immediately switch to mobile hotspot
- Or call recruiter: "My internet went down. Can we reschedule or do phone?"

**Computer Crashes:**
- Have phone ready to call/text recruiter
- Join from mobile device backup
- Stay calm - technical issues happen

### Interview Challenges

**You Blank on a Question:**
- "That's a great question. Let me think for a moment..." [3-second pause]
- Or: "Could you rephrase that? I want to make sure I answer what you're looking for."

**You Don't Know Something:**
- Be honest: "I haven't worked with that directly..."
- Pivot: "...but I have experience with [similar], and here's how I'd approach it..."

**Interview Running Over:**
- It's a GOOD sign! They're interested
- If you have a hard stop: "I do have a commitment at [time], but I'm happy to continue until then or schedule a follow-up."
